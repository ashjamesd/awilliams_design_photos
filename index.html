Design Documentation Photography — Guidecraft Industrial Design

This project involved freelance photography documenting the work of an industrial designer at Guidecraft. The goal was to capture both professional and personal product designs in a way that felt more exploratory and playful than traditional ecommerce photography.

Exploratory Product Photography

An industrial designer at Guidecraft wanted to document their work in a way that reflected the creativity and experimentation behind the design process. Rather than presenting products in a standard ecommerce format, the photography aimed to explore different compositions, lighting approaches, and visual storytelling techniques.

The resulting images focused on highlighting form, material, and interaction, while allowing the products to be presented in a more expressive and design-focused context. This approach helped create a visual archive of both professional and personal projects that felt more aligned with the designer’s creative process.
